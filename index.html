About CU Eventra

Transforming campus life through seamless event management

Revolutionizing Campus Events

CU Eventra is Chandigarh University's premier event management portal, designed to bridge the gap between event organizers and students. Our platform streamlines the entire event lifecycle from discovery to feedback.

Whether you're a student looking to enhance your campus experience or an administrator managing events, our intuitive system provides all the tools you need for success.
